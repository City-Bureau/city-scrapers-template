board meetings, notices, agendas, and minutes

Meetings of the URA Board of Directors are held on the second Thursday of each month at 2 p.m. in the Lower Level Conference Room at 412 Boulevard of the Allies, Pittsburgh, PA 15219.

Changes to the agenda may be made prior to the meetings.

Notice

Starting January 2020, the URA Board Meetings will be paperless. The full agenda and directors reports will be posted below on this page.

In an effort to reduce paper use, the URA asks that Board Meeting attendees view the meeting agenda on their phone or tablet, or print it from home prior to the 2 p.m. meeting time. Printed copies will be available for attendees upon request. Please contact Theresa Schacht at tschacht@ura.org by 11 a.m. the morning of the Board Meeting.

Thank you for your cooperation.
